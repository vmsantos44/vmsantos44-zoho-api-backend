## Official Onboarding Process
(From AlfaSystems_Onboarding_Process.pdf)

### 7-Stage Interpreter Onboarding Workflow

#### Stage 1: Application
- Candidates apply through recruitment form
- **Auto-response:** Email and SMS confirmation sent within 2-3 business days
- **Status in CRM:** "New Candidate" or "Application Received"

#### Stage 2: Interview
- **Format:** 15-minute screening call
- **Purpose:** Verify fluency, availability, equipment readiness
- **Conducted by:** Recruitment team members
- **Status in CRM:** "Interview Scheduled"

#### Stage 3: Tier Classification
**Official Tier Definitions (from Candidate Screening Process - November 2025):**

| Tier | Criteria |
|------|----------|
| **Tier 1 - Highly Qualified** | • Minimum 1 year of Over-the-Phone Interpretation (OPI) experience<br>• At least 40 hours of official interpreter training<br>• Professional certification (preferred)<br>• Ready for immediate deployment |
| **Tier 2 - Qualified** | • Minimum 1 year of formal on-site interpretation (hospitals, legal settings)<br>• Industry-standard interpretation training<br>• Can transition to OPI with 2-3 weeks training (40 hours) |
| **Tier 3 - Entry Level** | • Limited or no formal interpretation experience<br>• Minimal professional training<br>• Requires foundational training before assignment |

#### Stage 4: Assessment Phase
- Candidates complete language or interpretation assessments
- **Graded by:** Alfa Languages (partner assessment provider)
- **Types:** Language proficiency tests, interpretation scenarios
- **Status in CRM:** "Assessment Pending" or "Documents Pending"

#### Stage 5: Approval & Onboarding
**Includes:**
- Equipment check (headset, internet speed, quiet space)
- Language approval from Alfa Languages
- Contract acceptance (Independent Contractor Agreement)
- **Status in CRM:** "Onboarding"

#### Stage 6: Orientation
- Candidates receive onboarding details
- Access setup instructions provided
- Platform training materials shared
- **Status in CRM:** "Training" or "Onboarding"

#### Stage 7: Activation
- Approved interpreters converted to active status
- Added to Zoho CRM as active Contact
- Integrated into Alfa Systems scheduling databases
- **Final Status in CRM:** "Active Interpreter"
